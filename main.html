Paris, France


Paris, the capital city of France, is often called the "City of Light" and is famous for its rich culture, art, fashion, and iconic landmarks. Known for its romantic atmosphere and vibrant history, it remains one of the most popular travel destinations in the world.

Top 5 Attractions

  1. The Eiffel Tower – A global icon and a must-visit landmark.
  2. The Louvre Museum – Home to world-famous artworks like the Mona Lisa.
  3. Notre-Dame Cathedral – A masterpiece of Gothic architecture.
  4. The Champs-Élysées – One of the most beautiful avenues in the world.
  5. Sacré-Cœur Basilica – A stunning white basilica atop Montmartre hill.

Must-Try Foods

  • Croissants – Buttery and flaky, perfect for breakfast.
  • Macarons – Sweet and colorful French confections.
  • Coq au Vin – A classic French dish cooked with wine.
  • Ratatouille – A delicious vegetable medley with Provençal herbs.
  • Crème Brûlée – A creamy dessert with a caramelized top.
